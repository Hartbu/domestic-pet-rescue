DPR Launches New vet Tech Training Program

The new program will train and equip anyone interested in pursuing a career as a veterinary technician in our shelters and clinics. From assisting with exams and surgeries, to taking X-rays and administering medications, trainees will receive hands-on, formal training to turn their passion for animal welfare into the Domestic pet Rescue to a career with DPR.
